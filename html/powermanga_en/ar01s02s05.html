2.5. Description of the PLAY screen

01: Level of life of the guardian.

02: Level of life of the player.

03: Player’s score.

04: Score are multiplied by 2 or 4.

05: Change of Spaceship.

06: Rear shot Force 2.

07: Rear shot Force 1.

08: Right shot Force 2.

09: Right shot Force 1.

10: Left shot Force 2.

11: Left shot Force 1.

12: Front shot Force 1.

13: Front shot Force 2.

14: Spaceship repair.

15: Speed Booster.

The level of energy still available to run the Spaceship is circled with green (Mark 1 for guardians and 3 for your space ship).

The option that can be acquired is circled with yellow (Mark 11).
