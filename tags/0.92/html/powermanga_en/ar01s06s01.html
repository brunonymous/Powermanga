Since 1990, our company develops quality games and distribute on a cost price basis, or even less. We distribute our games as shareware or like this one as a freeware but we want to remunerate, even very modestly, our developers, graphic artists.

Without the support of the players, we simply cannot go on developing new games or even update our games to take advantage of the fantastic evolution of the technologies.

Even the smallest donation can help and we are thankful for any contribution.
