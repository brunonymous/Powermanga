5.1. Tests with 3D Accelerated Graphic Cards

During our trials, we have tested different graphic cards to be sure of their compatibility with the game. Because there are so many of them, we were unable to test them exhaustively. In addition, some of the manufacturers have now disappeared and can no longer offer technical support for their products. That the reason why we cannot guarantee that the game will work with any graphic card. We recommend strongly installing the most recent drivers of your video card.

If you have any problem with your graphic card or similar, we recommend installing the most recent drivers for the device. (Avoid using beta versions, they are generally less stable). Be sure also that your version of DirectX is the latest available, if it is not, download it from the Microsoft site on following URL: http://www.microsoft.com/directx/homeuser/downloads/default.asp
