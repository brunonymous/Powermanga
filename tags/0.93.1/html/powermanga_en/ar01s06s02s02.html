6.2.2. Donate by registering a Game

Go on the site http://www.tlkgames.com and open the tab “Games”.

Select a game you want to offer (for you or for a friend)

Click on the button “Add to cart”.

Place your securized order, check and validate it.

You will receive almost immediately on your e-mail the link to download your game and your bill.
